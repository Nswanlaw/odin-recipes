Steak and Eggs

Description

Steak and eggs is a classic American breakfast dish of fried eggs and beefsteak. It's often served with potatoes, toast, or hash browns. It can be made with a variety of side condiments like ketchup, hot sauce, salsa, and/or avacado. Flavor can also be added with herbs such as parsley, basil, or chives.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Steak
  • Salt & Black Pepper
  • Olive Oil, Avacado Oil, or Butter
  • Fresh Parsley (optional)
  • Avacado (optional)
  • Whole Grain Bread (optional)

Steps

  1. Dry and season the steak.Pat dry with paper towels. Add a small amount of melted butter or oil spread on to both sides. Sprinkle both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook the steak. Refer to a time chart for appropiate steak cook-time with regards to thickness and method (using a grill or stovetop). Let the steak rest on a cutting board while moving on to Step 3
  3. Cook the eggs. Cook using the desired method for eggs. While we prefer/recommend them "sunny side up", any method, such as scrambled may be utilized and enjoyed for this recipe.
  4. Serve the steak and eggs together. If you're adding hash browns, we recommended cooking them before the steak and let them sit in a covered container. If toast, we recommend toasting while cooking the eggs (during or just prior to Step 3). If adding avacado, we recommend pre-slicing or doing so while the eggs or steak is cooking. Add everything to your plate and enjoy.
